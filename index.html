The Challenge

Why Life Capsule?

In a rapidly ageing society, many seniors face the fear of dying alone or leaving behind unresolved end-of-life matters. We provide one unified sanctuary for your legacy and connection.

Combat the fear of dying unnoticed
Reduce burden on grieving loved ones
Organize administrative hurdles easily
Break social taboos through active planning

Our Vision

To redefine ageing by ensuring every individual remains connected, prepared, and valued throughout life.

重新定义老龄化，确保每个人在生命中始终保持联系、做好准备并获得尊重。

Our Mission
